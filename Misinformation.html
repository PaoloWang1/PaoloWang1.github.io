Information Transmission Methods on Social Media

Researched information transmission methods on social media at the 2023 Science Internship Program at UCSC. Specifically, we looked at the mediums through which information on Twitter and TikTok travels with thematic analysis. Some general themes we found were satire, facts and data, video edits, and relying on emotions.
